=== en_UK ===
Winesaps – Online Multiplayer Competitive Run PvP Game.

Challenge your friends to an online PvP race duel!
The goal is to collect all the food items on the battle field before your rival does the same.
But be careful! There are lots of wolves that want to "collect" both you and your opponent!
Moreover you may get equipped with different weapons and useful things, e.g. mine detectors, flashbangs, teleports and so on.
So pick up these items and turn the enemy's flank!

Features:
 - 4 characters
 - over 16 battle fields
 - over 10 additional abilities
 - different tactics to win
 - lots of collectible power-ups to impact on enemies
 - lots of obstacles (dais, waterfalls, mines, etc.)

Broadband network connection required.
